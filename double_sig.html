Origin

The name was inspired by Bloom's 2 Sigma Problem, which shows that a one-on-one tutoring environment is significantly more effective than a lecture environment. That is, if you compared a lecture class and a tutored class, the averaged tutored student would score 98th percentile of the lecture class. Double Sig tries to make the level of speed and compreshension associated with one-one-one tutoring a reality.
